Who we are

SPCA International

SPCA (Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals) International works to stop the abuse and suffering of animals with innovative programs that serve both animals and their human caretakers.

Donate

Make a donation today !

SPCA is a non-profit organization. We provide food, shelter, medical treatment and loving care until homeless animals can find a forever home. We rely on your support to save these wonderful animals.

Volunteer

Share your love

As a volunteer, you are helping SPCA save thousands of unwanted cats and dogs, giving them the opportunity to find loving new homes. Your contribution of time and effort is greatly appreciated.
